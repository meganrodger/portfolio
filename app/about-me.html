About me


I am a designer of public services, interested in how human behaviour and development interacts with and is affected by all forms of technology.

I am currently working at the Home Office Digital having completed the Interaction Design Internship program, though previously worked across graphic design, environment design and design installation.

I completed my masters in Interaction Design at Goldsmiths in December 2016, and BA Visual Communication at Glasgow School of Art in 2011.
